Our Profile

Dapo Olaiya Consulting is a growing firm of Estate Surveyors and Valuers established in 2004 to provide consulting services to the real estate industry, financial institutions, and the public sector in Nigeria. We are members of the Nigerian Institution of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (NIESV) and The International Facilities Management Associations (IFMA). We intend achieving this by creating Real Estate Business and Asset Valuation experience that exceed your expectations in hopes that you will refer Dapo Olaiya Consultant’s services to others. We are forward thinking, proactive and committed to placing our customers’ care at the core of our business culture.

Our Business-

We are committed to operate an ethical business in the real estate and valuation industry offering services that provide total satisfaction and/or exceed the expectations of all our clients

Quality to our Customer -

We value our clients as the foundation of our business. Our relationships with clients are based on principles of mutual benefit and respect. Our goal is to develop profitable and lasting relationships. We aim to build on what we do well and to innovate to meet changing needs of our clients

Quality to our Staff -

We respect the skills and experience of our staff and value the role that every person makes to the business team. Our goal is to work together to reward, train and develop our staff in ways that acknowledge performance and individual abilities.
